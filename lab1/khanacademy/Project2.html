Recipe #1: Creamy Pasta

  • Time: 50 min
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Sauce 1
Cream 1
Cheese 2
Pasta 1
Salt 2 spoons

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot and add salt.
  2. Add pasta and cook for about 10 minutes.
  3. Heat the sauce in a pan.
  4. Add cream to the sauce and mix well.
  5. Add cheese and stir until melted.
  6. Mix the pasta with the sauce.

Recipe #2: Fresh Salad

  • Time: 15 min
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Lettuce 1
Tomato 2
Cucumber 1
Olive oil 2 spoons
Salt 1 spoon

Steps:

  1. Wash all vegetables.
  2. Cut lettuce, tomato, and cucumber.
  3. Put vegetables into a bowl.
  4. Add olive oil and salt.
  5. Mix everything well.
